K-Items

The purpose of the project

K-pop is the most popular all over the world and there are many k-pop fans all over the world. This project aims to enable k-pop fans in Myanmar to easily purchase accessories related to their favorite idols and aims for they can purchase within the country. K-pop idol accessories store system permits a customer to submit orders for items and/or services from a store that serves online customers. In this each , K-pop idol related accessories can be purchase in one place. This project has been done by a team at school, and there are three members involved. I mainly wrote the frontend in this project.

Users will be able to view their own profile again.

Home Page

You will be taken to the home page with three groups.

Category

Users can choose their favorite group.

Popular Items

Items

Quantity

Users can enter the amount they want and purchase it.

Cancel or Confirm

Vouchers will be issued for all purchased products.
