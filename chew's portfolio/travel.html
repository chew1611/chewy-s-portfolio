Travelling to Japan

The purpose of the project

I wrote this project by myself. Nowadays, the tourism industry has expanded quite a lot.Most of the people come out for leisure. As transportation has improved, domestic and international travel services have become more and more popular.That's why I've written this to make it easy for people who are going on a leisure trip from Myanmar to Japan to book and stay. The people who are going to travel don't have to go to an agency to book and only use the Internet. If they book through this project, it will save time.In this project, hotel booking is also considered to make it easier for passengers to calculate.
